About Me

I’ve always been fascinated by how mathematics can explain patterns in the real world. During my journey at IIT Dhanbad, this curiosity gradually evolved into a passion for data science and artificial intelligence—because in today’s AI era, I believe technology should not only solve problems but also make life easier and more advanced.

My projects reflect this philosophy. Whether it’s building a multimodal assistant that allows users to interact naturally with AI for healthcare guidance, or developing a Property Investment Analytics Platform that helps people make smarter financial decisions, my focus has always been on designing solutions that directly fulfill user needs.

For example, on most real estate sites like 99acres, you’ll find property price and location details—but there’s little support in understanding whether a property is truly profitable or how it compares to others. That’s the kind of gap I aim to close through data-driven insights.

For me, data science is more than algorithms and models—it’s about bridging the gap between people and technology. I see myself as a lifelong learner and AI enthusiast, constantly exploring how intelligent systems can create real-world impact.
